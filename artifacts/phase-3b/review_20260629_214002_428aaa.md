### [CRITICAL] P0 prerequisite incomplete — SnapshotBuilder not threaded
File: workspace/trading/snapshot.py:429 (and slices at lines 248,254,262,264,
268,270,272,274,277,280,283,286,481)
Issue: The blueprint and the Executor's own contract require threading
`config: TradingConfig` through SnapshotBuilder, replacing the literals
`confluence_band_pct` (`* 0.001`, line 429), `recency_caps` (the `[-10:]`/
`[-5:]`/`[-3:]` slices), `pools_to_llm` (`[:5]`, line 481), `htf_tf_order`, and
`killzone_windows`. NONE of these were threaded — every literal is still
hardcoded and SnapshotBuilder never receives or reads a TradingConfig.
Consequently the config-accessors `TradingConfig.recency_caps_dict()` and
`killzone_windows_list()` are dead code (verified: no caller anywhere in
workspace/ outside config.py). Because the recording/Tier-2 path drives all
its variation through TradingConfig, an un-threaded SnapshotBuilder means a
non-default TradingConfig would silently NOT change snapshot behavior — Tier-2
"recording variants" would be a no-op for every snapshot-layer knob. P0 is the
hard gate that blocks all downstream work; it is not satisfied.
Fix: Thread `config` through SnapshotBuilder for all five fields; confirm the
unedited Phase 1/2/3A suite stays green; remove or wire the dead accessors.
